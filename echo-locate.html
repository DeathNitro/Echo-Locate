Echo-Locate v1.0

Navigate with calm.

A sensory-aware navigation app for neurodivergent individuals, those with PTSD, or anyone sensitive to noise and crowds.

🌿
Calm Routes
Routes avoiding crowded streets, loud areas, and overwhelming environments.
🔊
Text-to-Speech
Step-by-step audio directions so you never need to look at the screen.

Visual Impairment Mode
High contrast, large text, and full audio guidance optimised for low vision.
📍
Live Tracking
Real-time GPS tracking with on-screen position and upcoming turn alerts.
🗺️
Sensory Reports
See noise, crowd, and lighting levels at your destination before you go.
🚶
Multi-Transport
Walking, cycling, and transit routes — each optimised for your sensory needs.
